About

Hello Everybody, welcome to my space. I'm Evan, born and raised in Bed-Stuy, Brooklyn - I am African American with roots in Barbados. Now, I'm expanding my horizons over at Tokyo University of Technology, where I'm not just studying, but evolving. Being here is about more than academics; it's about channeling that hustle ingrained in me into something bigger. I've got a toolkit of talents, and my aim? To lift up those from my community, to spark change, and to contribute something meaningful back to the places that shaped me. Art and innovation are my playgrounds. I believe in the power of creation - whether that's through visuals, tech, or community work - to convey stories, challenge perceptions, and bridge divides. My mission is to create with purpose, to resonate and inspire. This website? It's a window into my world. Here, I plan to share not just my Talents, but the essence of my journey. So, I hope that through my services I can help those from my community. I'm here to make an impact, push boundaries, and leave a legacy.
